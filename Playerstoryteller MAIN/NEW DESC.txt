[h1]RAT LAB 2.0: THE SIDECAR UPDATE[/h1]

Turn RimWorld into a massive social experiment. Rat Lab allows your viewers to interact directly with your game through a web dashboard—spawning items, triggering raids, and managing the chaos—all with [b]zero performance impact[/b] on your game.

[h2]THE PERFORMANCE REVOLUTION: INTRODUCING "SIDECAR"[/h2]

Previous streaming mods slowed RimWorld down by forcing the game to compress video. Rat Lab 2.0 uses a revolutionary [b]Hybrid Architecture[/b]:

[list]
[*][b]ZERO-OVERHEAD CAPTURE:[/b] We no longer ask RimWorld to render the stream. A separate, lightweight "Sidecar" process captures your window directly. Your game runs at full speed while the Sidecar handles the heavy lifting.
[*][b]SMOOTH 30 FPS:[/b] By decoupling the stream from the game tick, your viewers get a buttery smooth video feed even when your colony is lagging from 500 tribal raiders.
[*][b]FIREWALL BYPASS:[/b] We have upgraded our networking to use standard secure web ports (WSS/443), meaning you can now host from strict networks like dorms or offices without port forwarding.
[/list]

[h2]FOR VIEWERS: GOD MODE ENABLED[/h2]
Viewers access your colony via [url=https://ratlab.online]ratlab.online[/url]. No Twitch account required.

[list]
[*][b]INTERACTIVE PINGS:[/b] Viewers can click anywhere on the video feed to create a visual "Ping" in your game, highlighting resources or danger.
[*][b]LIVE INVENTORY:[/b] See exactly what is in every stockpile and what every colonist is carrying, updated in real-time with item icons.
[*][b]FULL BIOGRAPHY:[/b] Inspect colonist traits, health conditions, and mood breakdowns without the streamer needing to click on them.
[*][b]REAL-TIME DASHBOARD:[/b] Monitor colony wealth, power consumption, and faction relations live in the browser.
[/list]

[h2]PRIVACY & TECHNICAL DISCLOSURE[/h2]
This mod includes a bundled executable ("stream-sidecar.exe") used exclusively for window capture and video encoding.

[list]
[*][b]Data Transmission:[/b] Game state (JSON) and Video (MP4 fragments) are transmitted to our relay server at ratlab.online via encrypted WebSocket (WSS).
[*][b]Persistence:[/b] No video is recorded or stored. Session data is held in RAM and wiped immediately when the session ends.
[*][b]Authentication:[/b] Sessions are secured via a unique Session ID and Stream Key generated locally on your machine.
[/list]

**RAT LAB 2.0: THE SIDECAR UPDATE & ROADMAP**

I have released a complete rewrite of the streaming architecture ("The Sidecar Update") to address performance overhead and connectivity issues. Additionally, I am unveiling the comprehensive roadmap for the next phases of development.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
**PART 1: THE UPDATE (LIVE NOW)**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

**The "Sidecar" Architecture**
I moved video encoding out of the Unity engine entirely. The mod now spawns a lightweight external process (`stream-sidecar.exe`, written in Go) that captures the game window directly via OS APIs.
* **Zero-Overhead:** RimWorld no longer handles video compression, significantly improving in-game FPS.
* **Plug & Play:** A standard FFmpeg library is fully packaged within the mod. No external downloads, installations, or configuration is required.
* **Decoupled Rendering:** The stream maintains stability even if the game client stutters during heavy load.

**Network & Connectivity**
I replaced the legacy port-based connection with standard WebSocket Secure (WSS) routing.
* **Firewall Bypass:** Traffic now routes over port 443 (standard HTTPS). This resolves connection failures for users on restrictive networks like university dorms or corporate offices without requiring port forwarding.

**New Features**
* **Interactive Pings:** Viewers can click the video feed to spawn visual markers in the game world.
* **Full DLC Support:** I added event triggers for Biotech (Bossgroups), Anomaly (Golden Cube/Death Pall), Royalty, and Ideology.
* **Streamer Panel:** Added granular controls for economy inflation, global cooldowns, and per-event toggles.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
**STREAMER & VIEWER SECURITY**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We take the safety of your hardware and identity seriously.
* **IP Masking (Relay Architecture):** Rat Lab uses a relay server architecture. Viewers connect to our cloud server, *never* directly to your computer. Your home IP address remains completely hidden, protecting you from DDoS attacks.
* **Session Isolation:** Every stream generates a unique, cryptographically secure Session ID and Stream Key. Even if your game crashes, only you can reconnect to your session.
* **Anti-Griefing Protocols:** The Dashboard includes strict Rate Limiting (default 30 actions/min), Input Sanitization to prevent chat injection attacks, and a "Panic Button" to instantly disable viewer interactions.
* **Data Privacy:** We do not store video. Session data is held in RAM and is wiped immediately after the session ends.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
**KNOWN ISSUES**
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

* **Stream Delay:** Additive delay may occur after very long streaming sessions (2+ hours). Restarting the session resolves this immediately.
* **Resolution Changes:** Resizing the game window or changing resolution mid-stream currently causes the Sidecar to crash. A fix for dynamic resizing is in development.
* **AMD/Intel Encoding:** While NVIDIA (NVENC) is fully supported, AMD and Intel hardware encoding is currently experimental. If you experience artifacts, please switch to CPU encoding in the launcher settings.
